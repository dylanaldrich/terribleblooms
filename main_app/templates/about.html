Tear open the warm belly of earth, plant a few seeds, or lay a bulb
and let’s see what human nature spews into the day,
or births sobbing into evening.

All it takes is a few minutes, and
seeds deprived of air and light,
then drowned and left below ground.

Only faintest warmth for comfort
in the cold hopeful bed
Ah Life, your garden reveals some awful secrets
and yields some Terrible Blooms.

A Friendship Garden

A Garden of Terrible Blooms is a passion project, born of two friends' love for theater: Sharon Yablon and Thomas Chan. Thomas has provided sound design and live music for two of Sharon’s themed, one-act productions: Stomach Contents (Zombie Joe’s Underground Theater, 2018), which was set in the desert, and 24 Hours On Sunset (indoor and outdoor locations, 2011), which was an evening of one-acts centered around the iconic Sunset Boulevard. They have also worked together on individual plays and performances over the years in a variety of locations.
